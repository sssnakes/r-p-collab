Salut, I'm Pierre. A French creative with an interest in art, design, and our future.

I've worked in the creative industry for 7 years. Full-time for advertising agencies like Havas Worldwide London and Saatchi & Saatchi Sydney. More recently, I've been freelancing for agencies in Paris and London.

I'm keen on projects involving conceptual thinking, art direction, photography and design. I believe in ideas that help brands make a cultural impact, in whatever shape or size. I've worked on multiple integrated campaigns and pitches. I once dressed up a teenager in a giant sloth suit and blew up the internet. A few months after that, I made what was recognised as the most innovative piece of work to come out of Australia that year.
